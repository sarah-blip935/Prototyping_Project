Core Disciplines

Three Specialties.
Singular Focus.

Each discipline at APEXORA is led by a dedicated fellowship-trained specialist. Patients benefit from focused expertise and seamless cross-specialty collaboration when complex cases require it.

🦷

Oral Surgery

Comprehensive management of dentoalveolar pathology — from wisdom tooth removal and bone grafting to pre-prosthetic surgery and oral lesion management — with tissue-sparing technique as standard.

→
⚙️

Implantology

Single-tooth to full-arch restoration, guided implant placement, immediate loading, and zygomatic solutions for compromised bone. Every case is digitally planned for predictable, lasting outcomes.

→
🩺

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery

Orthognathic correction, facial trauma, TMJ reconstruction, cleft repair, and oncologic resection. Our OMS team operates within a multi-disciplinary framework for complex craniofacial conditions.

→

Patient Journey

From Referral to Recovery

STEP 01
📋
Referral & Triage

We accept GP, dental, and self-referrals. Cases are reviewed within 48 hours and triaged by urgency and complexity.

›
STEP 02
🔬
Specialist Consultation

A dedicated specialist reviews your imaging, history, and goals. You receive a clear diagnosis and personalised treatment plan.

›
STEP 03
🖥️
Digital Planning

All surgical cases use CBCT-guided virtual planning. Implant positions, bone volumes, and surgical guides are prepared in advance.

›
STEP 04
🌿
Surgery & Recovery

Procedure, aftercare protocol, and follow-up are all managed under one roof. You are never passed between providers.
